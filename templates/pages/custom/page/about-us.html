§ 02 Section Two — The short version

Broken Tractor is a family-owned aftermarket parts company, founded in Baton Rouge, Louisiana in 2006. We started with one phone, a cardboard box of Case parts, and a stubborn belief that the people calling for parts deserved to talk to someone who actually knew the answer.

Twenty years in, that’s still the bet. 27 people. 20,000+ SKUs across two warehouses. 36+ brands. The same phone number we’ve had since day one — and a real person at the other end of it. The roster above is the rest of the story.

§ 05 Section Five — What we refuse to do
  • → We won’t sell you a part we wouldn’t put on our own machine.
  • → We won’t hide behind a chatbot when your job site is stopped.
  • → We won’t tell you a part fits when we’re not sure it does.
  • → We won’t bounce you between five people before somebody helps.
  • → We won’t tell you the part shipped when we don’t know that it did.
  • → We won’t ever be too big to answer the phone.
