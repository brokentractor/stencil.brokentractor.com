Where the CX31 fits in the Case excavator line

The CX31 is a 3.1-ton class Original-generation Case mini excavator. It sits in the compact tail-swing category - a class engineered for residential, landscape, utility, and tight-quarters work where a larger machine simply will not fit. As the Original-series foundation of the CX30/CX31 platform, it predates the later B-series and C-series revisions that brought updated emissions hardware and cab refinements.

Locating your serial number On the Case CX31 the serial number plate is located on the right-hand side of the lower cab frame, below the windshield (cab data plate). Confirm the serial before ordering year-specific parts - DPF, DEF/SCR, and ECU calibrations were revised through production.

Engine, hydraulics, and drivetrain

The CX31 runs a small compact diesel into a hydrostatic dual-path drivetrain with a rubber-track undercarriage. Boom, dipper, bucket, swing, and propel functions run from a main hydraulic pump with electrohydraulic or mechanical pilot control depending on configuration. The rubber-track undercarriage is purpose-built for the residential and landscape duty cycles where a steel-track machine would mark up turf or hard surfaces.

Tier 4 Final emissions compliance means DPF aftertreatment and, on equipped builds, DEF/SCR. The common-rail injection system demands clean fuel and tight water separation - the leading cause of unscheduled CX31 downtime is fuel contamination reaching the high-pressure injectors.

Common service items on the CX31

  • Engine internals: Compact diesel pistons, rings, sleeves, head gaskets. Common-rail injection.
  • Aftertreatment: DPF service intervals and regeneration behavior; DEF tank and dosing module, SCR catalyst; EGR cooler service on equipped builds.
  • Fuel system: Common-rail injectors, lift pump, primary and secondary filters with strict water-separator standards.
  • Cooling system: Radiator, water pump, thermostat, fan, charge-air cooler on turbo builds, hoses, belts.
  • Hydraulic system: Boom, dipper, bucket, and swing cylinder seal kits, main pump rebuild, electrohydraulic control valves, hoses.
  • Boom and dipper: Pin and bushing service at every joint, bucket pins, thumb attachment hardware, quick coupler service.
  • Undercarriage: Rubber track inspection and replacement, rollers, idlers, drive sprockets, recoil springs.
  • Swing system: Swing motor and reduction gear service, swing bearing inspection, swing brake.
  • Electrical / ECU: Engine ECU, instrument cluster, sensors, harness.
  • Cab glass and wiper: KHN16820 24-inch wiper blade, KHN3046 front lower glass, and KHN2632 front upper glass cross-fit across Case and Link-Belt excavators.
  • Operator station: Seat, joystick controls, pedal linkage, instrument panel, cab HVAC.

What to verify before ordering

  • Serial number. Confirm the machine serial before ordering year-specific parts.
  • Engine serial. Engine internal parts must match the engine serial number.
  • Hydraulic cylinder bore. Cylinder seal kit fit depends on bore dimension - measure before ordering.
  • Tier 4 emissions revision. DPF and DEF/SCR components saw revisions during production.
  • Engine ECU calibration. Common-rail injectors and ECU sensors are calibrated to specific build years.
  • Bucket and quick coupler. Quick-coupler interface varies - confirm before ordering attachment-side hardware.

Why the CX31 still earns a paycheck

The Case CX31 is sized for compact-excavator duty cycles where a 3.1-ton machine is the right tool - residential landscape, utility, irrigation, plumbing, and any tight-quarters digging where larger excavators will not fit. Compact footprint and rubber-track flotation suit residential and contractor work where a wheeled or larger tracked machine would mark up turf or fail to fit.

Maintenance priorities on a CX31: clean fuel and tight water separation for common-rail injection, DPF regen behavior tracking, hydraulic oil at temperature with clean filtration, rubber-track tension and wear monitoring, and pin and bushing service at every working joint.
