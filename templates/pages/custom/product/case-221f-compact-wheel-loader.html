Where the 221F fits in the Case wheel loader line

The 221F sits between the 121F and the 321F in Case's F-series compact wheel loader range. Same Tier 4 Final architecture as the rest of the F-series compact family, with chassis size and bucket capacity stepping up from the 121F.

F-series brought the compact Case loaders to EPA Tier 4 Final emissions with a refined common-rail diesel, DPF and SCR/DEF aftertreatment, modernized cab, and electronic engine controls. The 221F was built for landscape, light construction, ag yards, and small site work where a full-size loader is more machine than the job needs.

Quick specs and serial plate location

Locating your serial number On the Case 221F the serial number plate is on the left side of the machine near the front of the frame. Confirm the serial before ordering year-specific parts - F-series production saw running revisions to the engine, aftertreatment, and hydrostatic drive.

Engine, hydraulics, and drivetrain

The 221F runs a Tier 4 Final compact diesel through a hydrostatic drivetrain. Loader hydraulics use a separate priority circuit with cylinders sized for the 221F's bucket and tool-carrier work. The F-series cab brings improved visibility, HVAC, and electronic instrumentation. The ECU, aftertreatment, and common-rail injection define modern compact loader service practice.

The articulated frame steers through a steering cylinder at the pivot. Wet-disc brakes and hydrostatic deceleration handle stops. The tool-carrier interface accepts the standard compact-loader attachment family, with auxiliary hydraulic couplers for powered attachments.

Common service items on the 221F

  • Engine internals: Tier 4 Final compact diesel pistons, rings, sleeves, bearings, gaskets, head service.
  • Tier 4 aftertreatment: DPF service intervals, DEF tank and dosing module on SCR-equipped units, EGR cooler service, NOx sensors.
  • Fuel system: Common-rail high-pressure injection components, electronic injectors, lift pump, tight water-separator filtration.
  • Cooling system: Radiator, water pump, thermostat, fan, hoses, belts, charge-air cooler.
  • Hydrostatic drive: Closed-loop hydrostatic pump, wheel-end drive motors, charge pump, filters.
  • Loader hydraulics: Cylinder seal kits, hoses, control valves, auxiliary hydraulic couplers for attachments.
  • Articulation steering: Steering cylinder seal kits, articulation pivot pins and bushings.
  • Loader components: Bucket teeth, cutting edges, quick-coupler service.
  • Brakes: Wet-disc and hydrostatic deceleration service.
  • Electrical / ECU: Engine ECU, instrument cluster, sensors, wiring harness, telematics module. The 87528688 AC blower speed resistor fits across 521E through 1121F F-series cabs.
  • Cab / climate: F-series compact cab HVAC, filtration, controls, gauges.
  • Filter maintenance kits: Service kits are available bundled for F-series compact wheel loaders - confirm by model and serial before ordering.
