Where the CX235C SR fits in the Case excavator line

The CX235C SR is a 23.5-ton class C-Series short-radius excavator. It slots between the CX225 SR and the CX240 in Case’s full-size excavator lineup. The SR (short-radius) geometry keeps the counterweight inside the track width, making the CX235C SR ideal for confined-space utility, urban, and curbside work where a conventional tail-swing machine would foul nearby obstacles.

Engine, hydraulics, and drivetrain

The CX235C SR runs a Tier 4 Interim diesel through a hydrostatic dual-path drive into a steel-track undercarriage. The main hydraulic pump (load-sensing piston pump) drives boom, dipper, bucket, swing, and propel circuits through electrohydraulic control valves. The short-radius tail-swing geometry reduces the rear counterweight overhang for confined-space work - at the cost of slightly reduced swing stability when lifting at full reach.

Tier 4 Interim aftertreatment uses a DPF (diesel particulate filter) with periodic regeneration, cooled EGR, and on later-build configurations DEF/SCR dosing for NOx control. The C-Series cab is sealed and pressurized with a full instrument cluster, electrohydraulic joystick controls, and improved climate control versus the prior generation.

Common service items on the CX235C SR

  • Engine internals: Tier 4 Interim diesel - pistons, rings, sleeves, head gaskets. Common-rail injection.
  • Aftertreatment: DPF service intervals and regeneration behavior, DEF tank and dosing module, SCR catalyst, EGR cooler service.
  • Fuel system: Common-rail injectors, lift pump, primary and secondary filters with tight water-separator standards.
  • Cooling system: Radiator, water pump, thermostat, fan, charge-air cooler, hoses, belts.
  • Hydraulic system: Boom, dipper, bucket, and swing cylinder seal kits, main pump rebuild, electrohydraulic control valves, hoses.
  • Boom and dipper: Pin and bushing service at every joint, bucket pins, thumb attachment hardware, quick coupler service.
  • Undercarriage: Steel track inspection and replacement, rollers, idlers, drive sprockets, recoil springs.
  • Swing system: Swing motor and reduction gear service, swing bearing inspection, swing brake.
  • Electrical / ECU: Engine ECU, instrument cluster, sensors, harness, telematics module on equipped units.
  • Cab glass and wiper: Cab glass and wiper service items - KHN16820 24-inch wiper blade, KHN3046 front lower glass, and KHN2632 front upper glass cross-fit across Case and Link-Belt excavators.
  • Operator station: Seat, joystick controls, pedal linkage, instrument panel, cab HVAC.
Tier 4 Interim aftertreatment - service implications The CX235C SR’s DPF and (on later builds) DEF/SCR system are reliable when fed clean fuel and quality DEF, but they require attention. DPF requests periodic high-temperature regenerations, NOx sensors fail occasionally and can trigger derate conditions, and DEF quality matters - poor DEF damages SCR catalysts. Factor aftertreatment service into production maintenance budgets.

What to verify before ordering

  • Serial number. Confirm the serial on the CX235C SR before ordering year-specific parts.
  • Engine serial. Engine internal parts must match the engine serial number.
  • Hydraulic cylinder bore. Cylinder seal kit fit depends on bore dimension - measure before ordering.
  • Tier 4 emissions revision. DPF and DEF/SCR components saw revisions during production - confirm by serial.
  • Engine ECU calibration. Common-rail injectors and ECU sensors are calibrated to specific build years.
  • Bucket and quick coupler. Quick-coupler interface varies - confirm before ordering attachment-side hardware.

Why the CX235C SR still earns a paycheck

The Case CX235C SR is a 23.5-ton class production excavator suited to general site work, utility installation, residential and commercial earthmoving, and lighter mining/quarry work depending on configuration. The SR (short-radius) tail-swing geometry adds urban, curbside, and confined-space capability that conventional tail-swing machines can’t match.

Maintenance priorities on a CX235C SR: clean fuel and tight water separation for the common-rail injection system, DPF regen behavior tracking, hydraulic oil condition and filtration, swing-bearing lubrication, steel-track tension and undercarriage wear monitoring, and pin and bushing service at every joint.
