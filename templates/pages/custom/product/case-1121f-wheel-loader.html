Where the 1121F fits in the Case wheel loader line

The 1121F sits at the top of Case's F-series production wheel loader range - above the 1021F, the largest production-class machine before the G-series successor. F-series wheel loaders introduced Tier 4 Interim emissions with diesel particulate filter (DPF) aftertreatment, an updated cab platform, and electrohydraulic controls.

The 1121F shares cross-fit components across the F-series wheel loader family. Items like the 87528688 AC blower speed resistor fit across 521E through 1121F, and the G109152 steering cylinder bushing fits the 521F, 621, 721, and 821 series.

What defines the F-series at the top end

  • Tier 4 Interim emissions. Cooled EGR plus DPF aftertreatment for particulate control. No SCR/DEF on F-series Interim machines.
  • Power-shift transmission. Smooth modulation under load with electronic control.
  • F-series cab. Updated sealed-and-pressurized operator station, electrohydraulic loader controls on equipped configurations, color monitor display.
  • Largest F-series production chassis. Top-end bucket capacity above the 1021F and across-the-board the heaviest production unit in the F-series wheel loader range.
  • Wet-disc service brakes. Hydraulic master-and-slave actuation; cross-fit master and slave cylinders carry across multiple Case wheel loader generations.

Engine, hydraulics, and drivetrain

The 1121F runs a Tier 4 Interim Case diesel through a power-shift transmission with DPF aftertreatment. It is the largest cylinder geometry in the F-series wheel loader line, with proportionally sized loader hydraulics - piston-pump main supply, electrohydraulic control valves, and large-bore lift and tilt cylinders matched to the top-end bucket capacity.

The articulation steering circuit drives twin steering cylinders through the articulation joint. The drivetrain runs power-shift clutch packs, a power-shift transmission, drive shafts to front and rear axles, and planetary final drives at the wheels. Wet-disc service brakes provide hydraulic stopping at each axle.

Common service items on the 1121F

  • Engine internals: Case diesel pistons, rings, sleeves, bearings, gaskets. The R49482 engine motor mount is a recurring service item.
  • Tier 4 emissions: DPF service, EGR cooler service, differential pressure sensors, NOx sensors where fitted.
  • Fuel system: Common-rail injection components, electronic injectors, lift pump, tight water-separator filtration.
  • Cooling system: Radiator, water pump, thermostat, fan, charge-air cooler, hoses, belts.
  • Loader hydraulics: Cylinder seal kits, piston-pump rebuilds, electrohydraulic control valves, hoses.
  • Articulation steering: Steering cylinder seal kits, articulation pivot pins and bushings. G109152 steering cylinder bushing fits 521F, 621, 721, and 821 series.
  • Loader components: Bucket teeth, cutting edges, bushings, pivot pins, quick coupler hardware.
  • Drivetrain: Power-shift clutch packs, transmission components, axle seals, planetary final drives. A transmission shift lever assembly is a typical service item.
  • Brakes: Wet-disc service brakes. The S300718 brake shoe, A12985 slave cylinder, and N8695 master cylinder are recurring service items.
  • Electrical / ECU: Engine ECU, instrument cluster, sensors, wiring harness, telematics module. The 87528688 AC blower speed resistor fits 521E through 1121F.
  • Operator station: Seat, gauges, control levers, cab parts. 47880728 cab fresh-air filter on cab-equipped units.

What to verify before ordering

  • Generation matters. The 1121 series saw multiple generations - F vs. G has substantial part-number differences. Confirm by serial before ordering.
  • Machine serial. Confirm the serial on the left machine frame near the front axle before ordering year-specific parts.
  • Engine serial. Case diesel internal parts must match the engine serial number.
  • Aftertreatment configuration. DPF and EGR cooler part numbers can vary across the production run.
  • Transmission variant. Power-shift clutch and shift control parts can vary by build.
  • Cab vs. ROPS canopy. Operator station equipment varied across configurations.

Why the 1121F still earns a paycheck

The 1121F is the top of the F-series production wheel loader range. Production-class capacity, Tier 4 Interim emissions, F-series cab, and top-end bucket capacity make it a workhorse in quarry, aggregate, and heavy load-and-carry applications. Parts coverage is strong because the F-series shares many components across the 521F through 1121F range.

Maintenance priorities on a 1121F are clean fuel and tight water separation, DPF regen behavior, hydraulic system condition, and brake service. Broken Tractor maintains parts coverage for the 1121F alongside the rest of the F-series wheel loader family.
