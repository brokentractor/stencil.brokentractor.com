Where the 135 P-Tier fits in the John Deere excavator line

The 135 P-Tier sits just above the 130 P-Tier in the mid-class range - more dig depth and reach in the same operating-weight neighborhood. The P-Tier generation brings a refined cab, refined hydraulic management, and the long-running JD/Hitachi parts-sharing platform that makes service practical across the family.

Engine, hydraulics, and undercarriage

The 135 P-Tier runs a Tier 4 Final diesel through a hydrostatic drive into a steel-track undercarriage. The hydraulic family is the long-running JD 120C-135G platform - the PV7000 shim and seal kit covers that lineage end-to-end and applies to 135 P-Tier hydraulic service. For cooling, the FYA00013233 hydraulic oil cooler is a 135G direct-fit service item that carries into the P-Tier family.

The Tier 4 Final aftertreatment stack (DPF + SCR with DEF dosing) is standard equipment. Fleets running these machines should plan for periodic DPF regenerations, DEF top-ups, and NOx sensor service as part of normal operating cost.

Common service items on the 135 P-Tier

  • Engine: Tier 4 Final diesel internals - pistons, rings, sleeves, bearings, gaskets.
  • Aftertreatment: DPF, DEF dosing units, SCR catalyst, NOx sensors.
  • Cooling - oil cooler: FYA00013233 hydraulic oil cooler (135G direct fit).
  • Cooling - other: Radiator, water pump, hoses, fan.
  • Hydraulics: PV7000 shim and seal kit family (120C-135G). Main pump, control valve, swing motor, travel motors, boom/arm/bucket cylinders.
  • Cab glass: FYA00001495 upper windshield (130G/160GLC/180GLC cross-fit).
  • Undercarriage: Steel tracks, shoes, idlers, rollers, sprockets, track adjusters.
  • Electrical: Starter, alternator, ECU, monitor display, harnesses, sensors.

What to verify before ordering

  • Serial number. Confirm the serial on the upper frame before ordering year-specific parts.
  • Engine and aftertreatment serial. Tier 4 Final emissions parts are calibrated to specific engine serials.
  • Hydraulic seal kit family. PV7000 covers the 120C-135G family (including Hitachi EX100-ZX135).
  • Oil cooler cross-fit. FYA00013233 fits the 135G direct - confirm part-number match for P-Tier configuration.
  • Cab glass position. FYA00001495 is the upper windshield position; verify which pane needs replacement before ordering.
  • Undercarriage configuration. Standard vs. long-track variants affect chain, shoe, and roller part numbers.

Why the 135 P-Tier still earns a paycheck

The 135 P-Tier stays in service because contractors who need slightly more reach and dig than the 130 P-Tier - in the same operating-weight envelope - default to the 135. Same trailer, slightly more capability. That makes it a popular choice for fleets that already standardize on JD mid-class iron.

Maintenance priorities on a 135 P-Tier match the rest of the mid-class P-Tier family: clean fuel and DEF, on-schedule aftertreatment service, hydraulic oil cleanliness (PV7000 seal kit and FYA00013233 oil cooler service), steel-track wear management, and cab glass when the inevitable rock finds its target.
