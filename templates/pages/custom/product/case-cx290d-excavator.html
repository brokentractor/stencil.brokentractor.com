Where the CX290D fits in the Case excavator line

The material-handler configuration adds an elevating cab and grapple-friendly stick / grapple-end geometry to support scrap, recycling, and log-handling duty cycles.

Engine, hydraulics, and drivetrain

The CX290D runs a Tier 4 Final diesel through a hydrostatic dual-path drivetrain into a steel-track undercarriage. A load-sensing main piston pump drives boom, dipper, bucket, swing, and propel circuits through electrohydraulic control valves. The Tier 4 Final emissions package adds DPF, DEF/SCR aftertreatment, and an EGR cooler - all of which carry service implications not present on earlier B and C series machines.

The material-handler configuration uses an elevating cab to lift operator eye-line over piled material, and a stick / grapple-end geometry tuned for vertical reach and grapple work rather than digging. Swing system uses a hydraulic swing motor, reduction gear, swing bearing, and a holding brake.

Common service items on the CX290D

  • Engine internals: Tier 4 Final diesel - pistons, rings, sleeves, head gaskets, common-rail injection components.
  • Tier 4 Final aftertreatment: DPF service and regeneration, DEF tank and dosing module, SCR catalyst, EGR cooler service.
  • Fuel system: Common-rail injectors, lift pump, primary and secondary filters with tight water-separator standards.
  • Cooling system: Radiator, water pump, thermostat, fan, charge-air cooler, hoses, belts.
  • Hydraulic system: Boom / dipper / bucket / swing cylinder seal kits, main pump rebuild, electrohydraulic control valves, hoses.
  • Boom and dipper: Pin and bushing service at every joint, bucket pins, grapple/thumb attachment hardware, quick-coupler service.
  • Undercarriage: Steel track inspection and replacement, rollers, idlers, drive sprockets, recoil springs.
  • Swing system: Swing motor and reduction gear service, swing bearing inspection, swing brake.
  • Electrical / ECU: Engine ECU, instrument cluster, sensors, harness, telematics module on equipped units.
  • Cab glass and wiper: Cab glass and wiper service items - KHN16820 24-inch wiper blade, KHN3046 front lower glass, and KHN2632 front upper glass cross-fit across Case and Link-Belt excavators.
  • Operator station: Seat, joystick controls, pedal linkage, instrument panel, cab HVAC.
Tier 4 Final aftertreatment - service implications The CX290D's DPF and SCR systems require clean fuel and quality DEF to run reliably. DEF tank must stay topped up, DPF will request periodic high-temperature regenerations, and NOx sensors trigger derate conditions when they fail. Factor aftertreatment service into the maintenance budget on any production D-Series machine.

What to verify before ordering

  • Serial number. Confirm the machine serial before ordering year-specific parts.
  • Engine serial. Engine internal parts must match the engine serial number.
  • Hydraulic cylinder bore. Cylinder seal kit fit depends on bore dimension - measure before ordering.
  • Tier 4 emissions revision. DPF and DEF/SCR components saw revisions during D-Series production - confirm by serial.
  • Engine ECU calibration. Common-rail injectors and ECU sensors are calibrated to specific build years.
  • Bucket and quick coupler. Quick-coupler interface varies - confirm before ordering attachment-side hardware.
  • Material-handler vs. standard configuration. Elevating cab, grapple stick, and grapple-end geometry parts differ from standard excavator configurations.

Why the CX290D still earns a paycheck

The CX290D is a 29-ton class production machine in material-handler trim - scrap yards, recycling operations, log-handling, and any duty cycle that benefits from an elevated operator eye-line over piled material. The D-Series brought the platform to Tier 4 Final emissions while keeping the proven Case load-sensing hydraulic architecture and steel-track undercarriage.

Maintenance priorities on a CX290D: clean fuel and tight water separation for common-rail injection, DPF regen behavior tracking, DEF quality, hydraulic oil condition, swing-bearing lubrication, steel-track tension and undercarriage wear monitoring, and pin / bushing service at every joint.
