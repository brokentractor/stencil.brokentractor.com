Where the 160 P-Tier fits in the John Deere excavator line

The 160 P-Tier is the modern-emissions successor to the 160G LC in the JD mid-class excavator size range. The P-Tier branding indicates the current production generation in the JD construction equipment lineup, with Tier 4 Final emissions, an updated operator station, and refined hydraulic and engine management electronics.

The 16-ton size class is one of the volume sellers in the mid-class excavator market, and the JD/Hitachi platform parts sharing - which runs back through the G-Series and earlier C-Series machines - means service-part availability is broader than dealer-only. Many components cross-fit between 160DLC, 160GLC, and Hitachi EX/ZX siblings.

Locating your serial number On the John Deere 160 P-Tier the serial plate is on the right side of the upper frame, near the cab entry or adjacent to the operator’s seat. Verify the serial before ordering parts to ensure proper fit - running changes through production can affect part numbers.

Engine, hydraulics, and undercarriage

The 160 P-Tier runs a Tier 4 Final diesel engine through a hydrostatic drive into a steel-track undercarriage. Aftertreatment includes DPF for particulate control and SCR with DEF dosing for NOx control - both require service attention not present on the older C-Series machines.

Cross-fit service parts span back through the platform: PG200140 swing bearing covers 160DLC and 160GLC, AP38286-88 H-link fits 160DLC/160GLC and Hitachi EX150/EX160/ZX160, FYA00001495 upper windshield spans 130G/160GLC/180GLC, and FYA00001499 lower cab door glass covers 130G and 160GLC.

Common service items on the 160 P-Tier

  • Engine: Tier 4 Final diesel internals - pistons, rings, sleeves, gaskets, bearings.
  • Aftertreatment: DPF, DEF dosing units, SCR catalyst, NOx sensors, DPF differential pressure sensors.
  • Cooling: Radiator, hydraulic oil cooler, water pump, EGR cooler, hoses.
  • Hydraulics - seal kits: PV7001 shim & seal kit covers the 160C-160G family.
  • Hydraulics - other: Main pump, control valve, travel motors, swing motor, cylinders.
  • Swing: PG200140 swing bearing (160DLC + 160GLC), swing motor and gearbox.
  • Bucket linkage: AP38286-88 H-link (160DLC/160GLC + Hitachi EX150/EX160/ZX160), pins and bushings.
  • Cab glass: FYA00001495 upper windshield and FYA00001499 lower door.
  • Undercarriage: Steel tracks, shoes, rollers, idlers, sprockets, track adjusters.
Tier 4 Final aftertreatment - service implications The 160 P-Tier’s DPF and SCR systems are reliable when fed clean fuel and quality DEF. Keep the DEF tank topped up, allow periodic high-temperature DPF regenerations to complete, and replace NOx sensors promptly when fault codes appear - ignored aftertreatment faults will trigger engine derate.

What to verify before ordering

  • Serial number. Confirm the serial on the upper frame before ordering year-specific parts.
  • Engine and aftertreatment serial. Tier 4 Final parts are calibrated to specific engine serials.
  • Hydraulic seal kit family. PV7001 covers 160C-160G plus Hitachi EX150-ZX160 - confirm machine generation.
  • Swing bearing cross-fit. PG200140 fits 160DLC and 160GLC.
  • H-link cross-fit. AP38286-88 fits the wide JD/Hitachi 160 family.
  • Cab glass position. Upper windshield vs lower door - confirm location before ordering.
  • Undercarriage configuration. Standard vs long-track and shoe width variants exist.

Why the 160 P-Tier still earns a paycheck

The 160 P-Tier remains in service because the 16-ton P-Tier class is the modern volume seller in JD’s mid-class excavator line. Tier 4 Final fuel-efficiency improvements combined with a refined cab and controls make this the size class that fleet contractors rotate as their utility and commercial workhorse - small enough to move on a standard tag trailer, big enough to handle municipal and commercial-site production work.

Maintenance priorities on a 160 P-Tier: clean fuel and quality DEF, aftertreatment service intervals, hydraulic oil cleanliness (PV7001 seal kit family), swing bearing grease (PG200140), bucket linkage wear (AP38286-88), cab glass replacement, and standard undercarriage service.
