The John Deere 120C is the smallest C-Series mid-class JD excavator - a 12-ton class machine that marks the entry into the mid-class size range above the compact line. Where the 120C fits in the JD excavator line

The 120C is the smallest C-Series mid-class JD excavator - the entry into the mid-class size range above the compact and P-Tier compact line. The shared JD/Hitachi chassis means broad parts availability across the platform, with many components crossing into Hitachi ZAXIS equivalents and other C-Series Deere machines.

Engine, hydraulics, and undercarriage

The 120C runs a JD diesel through a hydraulic main pump into the boom, arm, bucket, swing, and travel circuits. A steel-track undercarriage carries the upper. Several direct-fit service parts are available at Broken Tractor: 4602562 upper windshield glass, 4602563R lower windshield glass, AP37371 engine compartment door RH, AP37370 engine compartment door LH, 9184497 swing bearing, AT317587 oil cooler, and PV7000 shim and seal kit.

Common service items on the 120C

  • Engine: JD diesel pistons, rings, sleeves, bearings, head gaskets, overhaul kits.
  • Cooling system: AT317587 oil cooler direct fit (120C + 160C LC). Radiator, water pump, fan, hoses, charge-air cooler.
  • Hydraulics: PV7000 shim & seal kit covers 120C-135G (Hitachi EX100-ZX135) hydraulic service. Main pump, control valve, swing motor, travel motors, cylinders.
  • Swing: 9184497 swing bearing (120C + 135C RTS shared). Swing motor, swing reduction gearbox.
  • Cab glass: 4602562 upper windshield + 4602563R lower windshield direct fit.
  • Engine compartment doors: AP37371 RH + AP37370 LH direct fit.
  • Undercarriage: Steel tracks, shoes, idlers, rollers, sprocket.

What to verify before ordering

  • Serial number. Confirm the serial on the upper frame before ordering year-specific parts.
  • Cab glass position. 4602562 is upper, 4602563R is lower - confirm position before ordering.
  • Engine door side. AP37371 is RH; AP37370 is LH.
  • Swing bearing cross-fit. 9184497 fits 120C + 135C RTS.
  • Oil cooler cross-fit. AT317587 fits 120C + 160C LC.
  • Hydraulic seal kit family. PV7000 covers 120C-135G (including Hitachi EX100-ZX135 equivalents).

Why the 120C still earns a paycheck

The 120C stays in service because it is the entry into the JD mid-class excavator size range at a price point and parts-availability level that smaller contractors can afford to own and maintain. The JD/Hitachi shared chassis means parts are available across multiple-make rebuilds, and the C-Series predates the heavier aftertreatment hardware that complicates later generations.

Maintenance priorities on a 120C: clean fuel on the JD diesel, hydraulic oil cleanliness (the PV7000 seal kit family covers the full Deere/Hitachi service envelope), swing bearing grease and rotation (9184497), oil cooler service (AT317587), and standard cab glass and engine compartment door replacement (4602562/4602563R/AP37371/AP37370 all direct fit).
