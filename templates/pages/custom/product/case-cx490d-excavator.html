Where the CX490D fits in the Case excavator line

The CX490D is a 49-ton class D-Series excavator, sitting between the smaller CX470C and the larger CX750D in Case’s full-size excavator family. The D-Series brought Tier 4 Final emissions compliance to the heavy class, with refined electrohydraulic controls, updated cab electronics, and an aftertreatment package built around DPF and SCR.

Engine, hydraulics, and drivetrain

The CX490D runs a Tier 4 Final diesel through a hydrostatic dual-path drivetrain into a steel-track undercarriage. The main hydraulic pump - commonly a load-sensing piston pump - drives boom, dipper, bucket, swing, and propel circuits through electrohydraulic control valves. Common-rail fuel injection and the DPF/SCR aftertreatment package require clean fuel and quality DEF to stay in service.

The undercarriage uses heavy-duty steel tracks with conventional rollers, idlers, sprockets, and recoil springs sized for the 49-ton class. The swing system pairs a high-torque swing motor and reduction gear over a sealed swing bearing.

Tier 4 Final aftertreatment - service implications The CX490D’s DPF and SCR systems are reliable when fed clean fuel and quality DEF, but they need attention. DEF must stay topped up, DPF regenerations need to be allowed to complete, and NOx sensors and DPF differential pressure sensors fail occasionally and trigger derate. Factor aftertreatment service into the maintenance budget on any production CX490D.

Common service items on the CX490D

  • Engine internals: Tier 4 Final diesel pistons, rings, sleeves, head gaskets, bearings. Common-rail injection components.
  • Tier 4 Final aftertreatment: DPF service and regeneration components, DEF tank and dosing module, SCR catalyst, EGR cooler.
  • Fuel system: Common-rail injectors, lift pump, primary and secondary filters with tight water-separator standards.
  • Cooling system: Radiator, water pump, thermostat, fan, charge-air cooler, hoses and belts.
  • Hydraulic system: Boom, dipper, bucket, and swing cylinder seal kits, main pump rebuild parts, electrohydraulic control valves, hoses.
  • Boom and dipper: Pin and bushing service at every joint, bucket pins, thumb attachment hardware, quick coupler service.
  • Undercarriage: Steel track inspection and replacement, rollers, idlers, drive sprockets, recoil springs.
  • Swing system: Swing motor and reduction gear service, swing bearing inspection, swing brake.
  • Electrical / ECU: Engine ECU, instrument cluster, sensors, harnesses, telematics module on equipped units.
  • Cab glass and wiper: KHN16820 24-inch wiper blade, KHN3046 front lower glass, and KHN2632 front upper glass cross-fit across Case and Link-Belt excavators.
  • Operator station: Seat, joystick controls, pedal linkage, instrument panel, cab HVAC.

What to verify before ordering

  • Serial number. Confirm the machine serial on the CX490D before ordering year-specific parts.
  • Engine serial. Engine internal parts must match the engine serial number.
  • Hydraulic cylinder bore. Cylinder seal kit fit depends on bore dimension - measure before ordering.
  • Tier 4 emissions revision. DPF and DEF/SCR components saw revisions during production - confirm by serial.
  • Engine ECU calibration. Common-rail injectors and ECU sensors are calibrated to specific build years.
  • Bucket and quick coupler. Quick-coupler interface varies - confirm before ordering attachment-side hardware.

Why the CX490D still earns a paycheck

The Case CX490D is a 49-ton class production excavator - general site work, utility installation, residential and commercial earthmoving, and mining and quarry work depending on configuration. It is a full Tier 4 Final machine with modern electrohydraulic controls and a cab that meets the operator-experience expectations of any current heavy excavator.

Maintenance priorities on a CX490D: clean fuel and tight water separation for common-rail injection, DPF regen behavior tracking, hydraulic oil condition, swing-bearing lubrication, steel-track tension and undercarriage wear monitoring, and pin and bushing service.
