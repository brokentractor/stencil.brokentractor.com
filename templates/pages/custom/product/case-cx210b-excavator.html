Where the CX210B fits in the Case excavator line

The CX210B is a 21-ton class B-Series excavator. It sits between the original CX210 and the later CX210C in Case’s full-size excavator lineup. The B-Series generation refined the platform’s hydraulic controls, improved engine efficiency under Tier 3 emissions, and upgraded the operator station for longer-day production work.

Engine, hydraulics, and drivetrain

The CX210B runs a Tier 3 diesel through a hydrostatic dual-path drivetrain into a steel-track undercarriage. The main hydraulic pump - typically a load-sensing piston pump - drives boom, dipper, bucket, swing, and propel circuits through electrohydraulic control valves. Conventional tail-swing geometry keeps the machine balanced for general earthmoving, utility, and site-prep work.

The B-Series hydraulic package is responsive at low engine speeds, which keeps fuel burn down and gives the operator finer modulation on fine grading and trench-bottom work. The dual-path drive lets the operator counter-rotate for tight-radius repositioning on confined sites.

Common service items on the CX210B

  • Engine internals: Tier 3 diesel - pistons, rings, sleeves, head gaskets, common-rail injection components.
  • Fuel system: Common-rail injectors, lift pump, primary and secondary filters with tight water-separator standards.
  • Cooling system: Radiator, water pump, thermostat, fan, charge-air cooler, hoses, belts.
  • Hydraulic system: Boom, dipper, bucket, and swing cylinder seal kits, main pump rebuild kits, electrohydraulic control valves, hoses.
  • Boom and dipper: Pin and bushing service at every joint, bucket pins, thumb attachment hardware, quick-coupler service.
  • Undercarriage: Steel track inspection and replacement, rollers, idlers, drive sprockets, recoil springs.
  • Swing system: Swing motor and reduction gear service, swing bearing inspection, swing brake.
  • Electrical / ECU: Engine ECU, instrument cluster, sensors, harness, telematics module on equipped units.
  • Cab glass and wiper: KHN16820 24-inch wiper blade, KHN3046 front lower glass, and KHN2632 front upper glass cross-fit across Case and Link-Belt excavators.
  • Operator station: Seat, joystick controls, pedal linkage, instrument panel, cab HVAC.

What to verify before ordering

  • Machine serial number. Confirm the serial on the CX210B before ordering year-specific parts - the B-Series run had running changes.
  • Engine serial. Engine internal parts must match the engine serial number.
  • Hydraulic cylinder bore. Cylinder seal kit fit depends on bore dimension - measure before ordering.
  • Engine ECU calibration. Common-rail injectors and ECU sensors are calibrated to specific build years.
  • Bucket and quick coupler. Quick-coupler interface varies - confirm before ordering attachment-side hardware.
  • Undercarriage configuration. Track shoe width and roller count can vary by build spec.

Why the CX210B still earns a paycheck

The Case CX210B is a 21-ton class production excavator suited to general site work, utility installation, residential and commercial earthmoving, and quarry or mining duty depending on configuration. It’s a balanced, well-supported platform that operators know how to run and parts people know how to support.

Maintenance priorities on a CX210B: clean fuel and tight water separation for the common-rail injection system, hydraulic oil condition and filtration, swing-bearing lubrication, steel-track tension and undercarriage wear monitoring, and pin and bushing service at the boom and dipper joints.
