Where the CX300C fits in the Case excavator line

The CX300C is a 30-ton class C-Series excavator. It sits between the CX290D Material Handler and the CX300D in Case's full-size excavator lineup. The C-Series brought Tier 4 Interim emissions controls, a refined operator cab, and updated electrohydraulic control valves over earlier Case full-size excavator generations.

Engine, hydraulics, and drivetrain

The CX300C runs a Tier 4 Interim diesel through a hydrostatic dual-path drivetrain into a steel-track undercarriage. The main hydraulic pump (typically a load-sensing piston pump) drives boom, dipper, bucket, swing, and propel through electrohydraulic control valves.

Aftertreatment on the C-Series includes a diesel particulate filter (DPF) with periodic regeneration cycles, EGR cooling, and on later builds, DEF/SCR components. The common-rail fuel system requires tight water separation and clean fuel - injector damage from contaminated diesel is the most expensive avoidable failure on these machines.

Common service items on the CX300C

  • Engine internals: Tier 4 Interim diesel - pistons, rings, sleeves, head gaskets, bearings. Common-rail injection.
  • Aftertreatment: DPF service intervals and regeneration behavior, DEF tank and dosing module, SCR catalyst, EGR cooler service.
  • Fuel system: Common-rail injectors, lift pump, primary and secondary filters with tight water-separator standards.
  • Cooling system: Radiator, water pump, thermostat, fan, charge-air cooler, hoses, belts.
  • Hydraulic system: Boom / dipper / bucket / swing cylinder seal kits, main pump rebuild, electrohydraulic control valves, hoses.
  • Boom and dipper: Pin and bushing service at every joint, bucket pins, thumb attachment hardware, quick coupler service.
  • Undercarriage: Steel track inspection and replacement, rollers, idlers, drive sprockets, recoil springs.
  • Swing system: Swing motor and reduction gear service, swing bearing inspection, swing brake.
  • Electrical / ECU: Engine ECU, instrument cluster, sensors, harness, telematics module on equipped units.
  • Cab glass and wiper: KHN16820 24-inch wiper blade, KHN3046 front lower glass, and KHN2632 front upper glass cross-fit across Case and Link-Belt excavators.
  • Operator station: Seat, joystick controls, pedal linkage, instrument panel, cab HVAC.
Tier 4 fuel and DEF quality matter Common-rail injectors on the CX300C don't tolerate contaminated diesel - keep the water separator drained and replace filters on interval. If the machine has DEF/SCR aftertreatment, use only ISO 22241-compliant DEF. Poor DEF quality damages the SCR catalyst and triggers derate conditions.

What to verify before ordering

  • Serial number. Confirm the serial on the CX300C before ordering year-specific parts.
  • Engine serial. Engine internal parts must match the engine serial number.
  • Hydraulic cylinder bore. Cylinder seal kit fit depends on bore dimension - measure before ordering.
  • Tier 4 emissions revision. DPF and DEF/SCR components saw revisions during production - confirm by serial.
  • Engine ECU calibration. Common-rail injectors and ECU sensors are calibrated to specific build years.
  • Bucket and quick coupler. Quick-coupler interface varies - confirm before ordering attachment-side hardware.

Why the CX300C still earns a paycheck

The Case CX300C is a 30-ton class production excavator suited to general site work, utility installation, residential and commercial earthmoving, and mining or quarry work depending on configuration. It runs in fleets across the country and parts support remains strong for the C-Series platform.

Maintenance priorities on a CX300C: clean fuel and tight water separation for common-rail injection, DPF regen behavior tracking, hydraulic oil condition, swing-bearing lubrication, steel-track tension and undercarriage wear monitoring, and pin and bushing service at every joint.
