John Deere 200C LC Excavator: C-Series 20-Ton LC Excavator

The John Deere 200C LC is a 20-ton mid-size excavator built for construction, demolition, and utility work. Produced from 2002 to 2007, the C-Series refined the cab and hydraulics over the 200LC while keeping the mechanical-era simplicity that mid-size contractors still depend on today.

Where the 200C LC fits in the John Deere excavator line

The 200C LC is the C-Series successor to the 200LC - refined cab, refined hydraulics, same 20-ton LC (long-carriage) class. It sits above the 160C LC and below the larger C-Series machines in the JD construction excavator family. C-Series production ran 2002 through 2007 before the D-Series took over.

Engine, hydraulics, and undercarriage

The 200C LC runs a JD diesel through a hydrostatic main pump that feeds the boom, arm, bucket, swing, and travel circuits. The undercarriage is the long-carriage steel-track LC configuration that gives the 200C LC its stability advantage over the standard-carriage variant.

Key direct-fit and cross-fit service parts for the 200C LC include the 9186437 track recoil and adjuster assembly (fits 200CLC, 200DLC, and Hitachi ZX-series), the 4602563R lower windshield glass (cross-fits 120C, 160CLC, 180CW, and 200CLC), and the 4333459 A/C compressor (cross-fits 200LC, 230LC, and 230LCRD).

Common service items on the 200C LC

  • Engine: JD diesel internals - pistons, rings, sleeves, bearings, gasket sets.
  • Cooling: Radiator, water pump, fan, hoses, thermostat.
  • A/C system: 4333459 A/C compressor direct cross-fit, condenser, dryer, expansion valve.
  • Hydraulics: Main pump, control valve, travel motors, swing motor, boom/arm/bucket cylinders, seal kits.
  • Undercarriage - recoil: 9186437 track recoil and adjuster direct fit.
  • Undercarriage - other: Steel tracks, shoes, idlers, rollers, sprockets.
  • Cab glass: 4602563R lower windshield cross-fit, upper glass, door glass.
  • Swing: Swing bearing, swing motor, swing gearbox.
  • Electrical: Starter, alternator, harnesses, switches, monitor display.

What to verify before ordering

  • Serial number. Confirm the serial on the upper frame before ordering year-specific parts. C-Series machines had running changes 2002-2007.
  • Production year band. The 200C LC ran 2002-2007. Earlier and later machines belong to different generations.
  • Undercarriage recoil cross-fit. The 9186437 fits 200CLC, 200DLC, and Hitachi ZX-series excavators.
  • A/C compressor cross-fit. The 4333459 fits 200LC, 230LC, and 230LCRD machines.
  • Cab glass position. The 4602563R is the lower windshield position - verify against the existing glass before ordering.
  • Carriage variant. 200C LC is the long-carriage variant. Standard-carriage parts will not interchange on UC and frame items.

Why the 200C LC still earns a paycheck

The 200C LC stays in active service because mid-2000s 20-ton C-Series JD excavators land in the sweet spot for mid-size contractors. They buy off-warranty, run them to high hours, and benefit from the refined cab and controls over the 200LC paired with mechanical-era hydraulic simplicity (no Tier 4 Final aftertreatment to maintain).

Maintenance priorities on a 200C LC: clean fuel and hydraulic oil, undercarriage wear (9186437 recoil and adjuster service on schedule), A/C system condition (4333459 compressor is the common failure), swing bearing greasing, and cab glass replacement when the lower windshield (4602563R) cracks.
