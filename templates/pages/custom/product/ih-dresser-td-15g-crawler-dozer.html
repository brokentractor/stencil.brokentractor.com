Where the TD-15G fits in the IH/Dresser line

The TD-15G is the G-series Dresser TD-15 - successor to the TD-15E. The TD-15 platform began life under International Harvester and carried forward under the Dresser brand after IH divested its construction equipment business. By the G-generation, the platform had matured into a heavy mid-to-large crawler dozer with hydrostatic drive, well-suited to production earthmoving, rough grading, and heavy land-clearing work.

The TD-15G shares undercarriage and engine architecture with its siblings up and down the Dresser heavy crawler line, which keeps parts availability strong across the IH/Dresser ecosystem.

What defines the G-series TD-15

  • Refined Dresser heavy diesel. The G-series engine carried forward the proven IH/Dresser heavy diesel architecture with refinements to fuel system, cooling, and electrical management.
  • Hydrostatic drive with G-series control logic. The G-generation hydrostatic transmission improved low-speed control and counterrotation response over the E-series.
  • Heavy-class chassis and blade. The TD-15G uses a heavy-class mainframe and blade group sized for sustained production work, not light grading.
  • Operator station improvements. Sealed and pressurized cab with revised seat, controls, and instrumentation.
Locating your serial number On the Dresser TD-15G, the serial number plate is on the operator-platform ID plate. Confirm the machine serial AND the engine serial before ordering year-specific parts. IH-era and Dresser-era machines saw multiple running revisions through the production run.

Engine, hydraulics, and drivetrain

The TD-15G runs a refined IH/Dresser heavy diesel through a hydrostatic transmission with G-series control logic. The engine family is one of the most-supported heavy diesels in the IH/Dresser parts ecosystem - head gaskets, pistons, sleeves, and bearings remain available through cross-fit components.

The hydraulic system powers blade lift, tilt, and angle functions through a main hydraulic pump and dedicated cylinders. The undercarriage is the heavy-class TD-15 chain with sealed-and-lubricated track, planetary final drives, and TD-pattern rollers, idlers, and sprockets.

Common service items on the TD-15G

  • Engine internals: IH/Dresser heavy diesel pistons, rings, sleeves, head gaskets. The S.57678 head gasket set cross-fits across the IH/Dresser heavy diesel family.
  • Cooling system: 3132738R93 water pump cross-fits IH/Dresser dozers. Radiator, hoses, thermostat, and fan drive service.
  • Hydraulic system: Main hydraulic pump (623084C92 cross-fit), 620630C92 blade angle cylinder, blade lift and tilt cylinder service, hoses, and filtration.
  • Hydrostatic drive: Drive pump and motor rebuilds, charge pump components, case drain hoses, filter housings.
  • Steering brakes: Heavy-duty wet-disc brake service - discs, plates, seals, actuators.
  • Operator station: PV862 seat package for IH/Dresser dozers and loaders, 621619C1 bottom seat cushion.
  • Blade: Cutting edges, mounting hardware (5J4773 cutting edge bolt - Dresser/Case/CAT cross-fit), pivot pins, and bushings.
  • Undercarriage: Heavy-class track chains, rollers, idlers, sprockets, recoil springs, and track adjusters.
  • Electrical: Starter, alternator, gauges, switches, harnesses.

What to verify before ordering

  • Machine serial number. Confirm the serial on the TD-15G before ordering year-specific parts - running changes occurred through the production run.
  • Engine serial. Internal engine parts (pistons, sleeves, bearings, head gaskets) must match the engine serial number.
  • Generation. IH-era and Dresser-era machines saw multiple revisions - confirm by serial before ordering hydraulic and drivetrain components.
  • Undercarriage spec. Track shoe count, roller spacing, and idler configuration vary by build - measure your existing components before ordering replacements.
  • Hydrostatic generation. G and H-series hydrostatic transmissions saw revisions - confirm pump/motor part numbers against existing tags before ordering.
  • Blade configuration. Straight, angle, and 6-way blade configurations use different cylinder specifications.

Why the TD-15G still earns a paycheck

The TD-15G is the G-series heavy-duty Dresser crawler - the platform that owners and small fleets keep running for earthmoving, grading, and land clearing at the top of the mid-large class. The IH/Dresser heavy diesel is one of the most-supported engines in the segment, and the TD-15 undercarriage shares parts across the broader Dresser heavy crawler line, which keeps replacement components available decades after the machine left the factory.

Maintenance priorities on a TD-15G: clean fuel, scheduled hydrostatic system service (fluid, filters, and pump/motor monitoring), heavy-class undercarriage monitoring (chain stretch, roller and idler wear), and proactive engine service to keep the diesel running at the duty cycle the machine was built for.
