Where the 885 fits in the Case motor grader line

The Case 885 is the heavy-class member of the Case 845 / 865 / 885 motor grader family. Articulated chassis, turbocharged Case diesel power, full-width moldboard with hydraulic circle drive. Road maintenance contractors, county road crews, and grading specialists run the 885 for finish work and ditch maintenance where the smaller 845 / 865 don’t have enough blade pressure or operating weight.

Engine, drivetrain, and circle / moldboard

The 885 runs a Case turbocharged diesel through the Case motor-grader powershift transmission into the rear tandem drive — with optional front-wheel drive on some configurations giving six-wheel drive. Articulated frame pivots between the front (operator + moldboard + circle) and rear (engine + tandem drive) for tight-turning grading work. Hydraulic circle drive rotates the moldboard 360° for windrow, ditch, and slope work. 87522165 Sanden SD7H15 A/C compressor (125mm 2-groove 24V) and 75252037 Sanden SD7H15 (132mm) fit Case 845/865/885 motor graders direct.

Common service items on the 885

  • Engine internals: Case turbocharged diesel pistons, rings, sleeves, bearings, head gaskets, full overhaul kits.
  • Cooling system: Radiator, water pump, thermostat, fan, hoses, belts, charge-air cooler.
  • Powershift transmission: Clutch packs, transmission filters and oil, torque converter service.
  • Drivetrain — tandem: Rear tandem chain cases, tandem hubs, planetary final drives, tandem oil service.
  • Drivetrain — FWD (if equipped): Front-wheel-drive hydraulic motors, hubs, hoses, control valves.
  • Hydraulics — moldboard / circle: Moldboard lift cylinders, side-shift cylinder, blade-tip cylinder, circle drive motor and pinion, circle teeth.
  • Articulation: Articulation joint pivot pins, bushings, steering cylinder seal kits.
  • Brakes: Wet-disc service brakes, brake oil filters, park brake actuation.
  • A/C system: Case 845/865/885 share the same A/C platform. Direct-fit service parts: 87522165 Sanden SD7H15 (125mm) and 75252037 Sanden SD7H15 (132mm) compressors, 75257955 compressor-to-condenser hose, 75257956 receiver-drier to expansion-valve hose, 75327255 1-groove compressor belt 40.2 inch, 75258258 compressor belt, 75251998 receiver-drier.
  • Cab and electrical: Cab HVAC blower, ECU (later units), instrument cluster, sensors, wiring harness, alternator, starter.

What to verify before ordering

  • Serial number. Confirm the serial on the frame rail or the cab data plate before ordering year-specific moldboard, circle, or articulation parts.
  • Engine serial. Case turbo diesel internal parts are calibrated to specific engine serial groups — confirm separately from the chassis serial.
  • Sanden compressor size. Sanden SD7H15 A/C compressor body comes in 125mm and 132mm clutch-pulley sizes (87522165 vs 75252037). Confirm pulley diameter and clutch interface before ordering.
  • A/C belt length. Two A/C belt service parts apply (75327255 1-groove 40.2″ and 75258258). Confirm belt configuration on the machine before ordering.
  • FWD option. Some 885 units have hydraulic front-wheel drive — confirm whether the unit is 6-wheel-drive equipped before ordering FWD-specific hydraulic motor or hub parts.
  • Tandem chain case revision. Tandem chain cases saw running revisions — confirm tandem assembly part numbers by chassis serial.

Why the 885 still earns a paycheck

The Case 885 stays in service because purpose-built motor graders earn their keep on road maintenance, ditch work, snow removal, and finish grading — work where loaders and dozers can’t deliver the moldboard precision and full-width pass that a motor grader does. Articulated frame gives the maneuverability needed for tight curves and finish work, and the turbo diesel delivers the moldboard pressure needed for heavy material.

Maintenance priorities on a 885: clean fuel and quality filtration on the turbo diesel, tandem oil service (tandems run heavy and need clean oil), circle drive lubrication and circle-tooth condition, articulation pivot wear, moldboard cylinder seals, and cab A/C system condition (Sanden SD7H15 compressor + belts + hoses + receiver-drier all stock at brokentractor.com under part numbers 87522165 / 75252037 / 75257955 / 75257956 / 75327255 / 75258258 / 75251998).
