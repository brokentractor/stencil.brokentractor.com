Where the 321F fits in the Case wheel loader line

The 321F sits at the top of Case's F-series compact wheel loader range - above the 221F and below the mid-size 421G in the G-series mid-size segment. Same Tier 4 Final compact architecture as the rest of the F-series compact family, with chassis size and bucket capacity pushed to the top of the compact class.

Engine, hydraulics, and drivetrain

The 321F runs a Tier 4 Final compact diesel through a hydrostatic drivetrain - bigger pump and motor capacity than the 221F. Loader hydraulics use a high-flow priority circuit with cylinders sized for the 321F's larger bucket and breakout. F-series compact cab - visibility, HVAC, electronic instrumentation, telematics integration.

Common service items on the 321F

  • Engine internals: Tier 4 Final compact diesel: pistons, rings, sleeves, bearings, gaskets, head service.
  • Tier 4 aftertreatment: DPF service intervals, DEF tank and dosing module on SCR-equipped units, EGR cooler service.
  • Fuel system: Common-rail high-pressure injection components, electronic injectors, lift pump, very tight water-separator filtration.
  • Cooling system: Radiator, water pump, thermostat, fan, hoses, belts, charge-air cooler.
  • Hydrostatic drive: Closed-loop hydrostatic pump, wheel-end drive motors, charge pump, filters.
  • Loader hydraulics: Cylinder seal kits, hoses, control valves, auxiliary hydraulic couplers for attachments.
  • Articulation steering: Steering cylinder seal kits, articulation pivot pins and bushings.
  • Loader components: Bucket teeth, cutting edges, quick-coupler service.
  • Brakes: Wet-disc and hydrostatic deceleration service.
  • Electrical / ECU: Engine ECU, instrument cluster, sensors, wiring harness, telematics module. The 87528688 AC blower speed resistor fits across 521E through 1121F F-series cabs.
  • Cab / climate: F-series compact cab - HVAC, filtration, controls, gauges.

What to verify before ordering

  • Serial number. Confirm the serial before ordering year-specific parts.
  • Engine ECU calibration. Common-rail injectors and ECU sensors are calibrated to specific build years - confirm by serial and ECU revision.
  • Tier 4 emissions revision. DPF, DEF, and SCR components saw revisions during F-series production.
  • Hydrostatic generation. Hydrostatic pumps and drive motors saw revisions during F-series production - confirm by serial.
  • Quick coupler. Compact wheel loader quick couplers vary by configuration - confirm tool-carrier interface before ordering attachment-side hardware.

Why the 321F still earns a paycheck

The 321F is the largest of Case's F-series compact wheel loaders - the right size when a contractor needs more bucket and breakout than a 221F but doesn't want to step into a mid-size G-series machine. F-series Tier 4 emissions, hydrostatic drive, F-series cab.

Maintenance priorities on a 321F: clean fuel and tight water separation, DPF regen behavior and DEF quality, hydrostatic charge pressure, and brake service when actuation softens. Broken Tractor maintains parts coverage across the Case wheel loader family.
