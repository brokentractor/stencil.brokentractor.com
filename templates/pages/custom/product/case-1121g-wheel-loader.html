Where the 1121G fits in the Case wheel loader line

The 1121G is the current top of Case's production wheel loader range - successor to the 1121F and seated above the 1021G. The largest production-class wheel loader in Case's current lineup, the 1121G is built for high-cycle quarry loading, large aggregate operations, and major construction sites where bucket capacity and breakout force matter.

The G-series brought Case's Tier 4 Final emissions package, refined operator station, full electronic drivetrain integration, and updated telematics across the production-class wheel loader range. On the 1121G that platform is paired with the largest frame, the heaviest counterweight, and the deepest bucket capacity in Case's current lineup.

What defines the G-generation 1121

  • Engine. Tier 4 Final Case diesel with electronic engine control, cooled EGR, and common-rail injection. The G-series engine package replaced the F-series Tier 4 Interim layout with a full Tier 4 Final solution.
  • Aftertreatment. Full DPF + DEF + SCR. The DEF tank and dosing module are routine service items, and NOx sensor failures can drive derate conditions if not addressed.
  • Drivetrain. Power-shift transmission with electronic shift control, locking differentials available on equipped configurations.
  • Operator station. G-series cab with updated HVAC, color monitor display, refined joystick controls, and integrated telematics.
  • Hydraulics. Variable-displacement piston pump driving the loader circuit through electrohydraulic main control valves.
Tier 4 Final aftertreatment - service implications The 1121G runs the full Case Tier 4 Final aftertreatment stack: DPF, DEF dosing module, SCR catalyst, NOx sensors fore and aft of the SCR, EGR cooler. Clean fuel, quality DEF, and on-schedule DPF service are not optional on this platform - skipping them invites derate codes and expensive aftertreatment component replacement.

Engine, hydraulics, and drivetrain

The 1121G runs a Tier 4 Final Case diesel through a power-shift transmission with full DPF + DEF + SCR aftertreatment. The engine drives a variable-displacement piston pump for the loader circuit, a separate steering pump for the articulated steering circuit, and the power-shift transmission feeds heavy-duty axles with planetary final drives at the wheel ends.

The articulated frame is steered by twin hydraulic cylinders. Service brakes are wet-disc and integrated into the axle housings. The cab is sealed, pressurized, and climate-controlled, with electronic monitoring of engine, drivetrain, hydraulic, and aftertreatment status routed through a color monitor display.

Common service items on the 1121G

  • Engine internals: Case diesel pistons, rings, sleeves, bearings, gaskets. R49482 engine motor mount is a recurring service item.
  • Aftertreatment: DPF, DEF tank and dosing module, SCR catalyst, NOx sensors, EGR cooler service.
  • Fuel system: Common-rail injection components, electronic injectors, lift pump, tight water-separator filtration.
  • Cooling system: Radiator, water pump, thermostat, fan, charge-air cooler, hoses, belts.
  • Loader hydraulics: Cylinder seal kits, piston-pump rebuilds, electrohydraulic control valves, hoses.
  • Articulation steering: Steering cylinder seal kits, articulation pivot pins and bushings. G109152 steering cylinder bushing fits the 521F, 621, 721, and 821 series.
  • Loader components: Bucket teeth, cutting edges, bushings, pivot pins, quick coupler.
  • Drivetrain: Power-shift clutch packs, transmission, axle seals, planetary final drives. Transmission shift lever assembly available for service.
  • Brakes: Wet-disc service brakes. S300718 brake shoe, A12985 slave cylinder, N8695 master cylinder are recurring service items.
  • Electrical / ECU: Engine ECU, instrument cluster, sensors, wiring harness, telematics module. 87528688 AC blower speed resistor fits 521E through 1121F.
  • Operator station: Seat, gauges, control levers, cab parts. 47880728 cab fresh-air filter on cab-equipped units.

What to verify before ordering

  • Generation matters. The 1121-series spans F-Tier 4 Interim and G-Tier 4 Final platforms - confirm by serial before ordering aftertreatment or ECU-dependent parts.
  • Serial number. Confirm the serial on the left machine frame near the front axle before ordering year-specific parts.
  • Engine serial. Case Tier 4 Final diesel internal parts must match the engine serial number.
  • Aftertreatment configuration. DPF, SCR, and DEF dosing part numbers can vary across the G-series production run. Confirm against existing aftertreatment serial tags.
  • Cab vs. ROPS canopy. Operator station equipment varied between configurations.
  • Telematics package. Equipped vs. non-equipped configurations affect cab harnesses and the telematics module part number.

Why the 1121G still earns a paycheck

The 1121G is Case's current top-of-line production wheel loader. Tier 4 Final emissions, DEF dosing, telematics, G-series cab - the largest production class in Case's current lineup. It is the machine Case customers running quarry, aggregate, and major-construction operations are buying new today.

Maintenance priorities on a 1121G are simple in concept and demanding in practice: clean fuel and tight water separation, DPF / DEF / SCR service per the Case interval schedule, hydraulic system condition with clean cool oil, wet-disc brake service, and articulation pivot bushing inspection. Skip any of those and the machine punishes you with derate codes, hydraulic heat, or articulation slop.
