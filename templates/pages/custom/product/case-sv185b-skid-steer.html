Where the SV185B fits in the Case skid steer line

The SV185B sits in Case’s alpha-series SV (vertical-lift) skid steer range between the Case SV185 and the Case SV250. It carries vertical-lift loader geometry, Tier 4 Final aftertreatment, the alpha-series cab, and electrohydraulic joystick controls. The vertical-lift arc keeps the bucket parallel to the chassis through the full lift cycle — the right geometry for truck-loading, stacking pallets, and material handling where forward reach at full height matters more than radial-lift’s ground-level dig pattern.

Engine, hydraulics, and drivetrain

The SV185B runs a Tier 4 Final compact diesel through a hydrostatic dual-path drivetrain. The engine uses common-rail high-pressure fuel injection, EGR, and a DPF/SCR aftertreatment package with DEF dosing for NOx control. Loader hydraulics use a load-sensing piston pump with electrohydraulic control valves driven by the joystick controls.

The vertical-lift loader geometry keeps the bucket parallel to the chassis through the lift arc — best matched to truck-loading, stacking, and material handling where forward reach at full height matters. The drivetrain is hydrostatic dual-path: independent pump and motor on each side feeding the drive chain to the wheels.

  • Engine internals: Tier 4 Final compact diesel — pistons, rings, sleeves, bearings, gaskets. Common-rail high-pressure injection on Tier 4 builds.
  • Tier 4 aftertreatment: DPF service intervals, DEF tank and dosing module, SCR catalyst, EGR cooler service.
  • Fuel system: Common-rail injectors, lift pump, very tight water-separator filtration.
  • Cooling system: Radiator, water pump, thermostat, fan, charge-air cooler, hoses, belts.

  • Drivetrain: Hydrostatic dual-path pumps and drive motors, charge pump, filters.
  • Bucket coupler hardware: 47396814K bucket pin kit cross-fits L216–L230, C232–C238, SR160–SR250, SV185–SV300, TV380, TR270–TR340 — high-volume coupler service kit.
  • Hardware: 191048A2 axle stud & nut cross-fits broadly across the Case skid steer line.
  • Brakes / control: Park brake, control linkage, 48139268 control shock absorber on Case/NH alpha-series control valves.
  • Electrical / ECU: Engine ECU, machine controller, instrument cluster, sensors, wiring harness, telematics module.
  • Cab / operator station: Cab HVAC, seat, gauges, joystick controls, cab glass, 47405956 windshield wiper blade on cab-equipped units.

What to verify before ordering

  • Aftertreatment generation. Tier 4 Final configuration varies by build year — confirm DPF/DEF/SCR layout before ordering.
  • Serial number. Confirm the serial on the Case SV185B before ordering year-specific parts.
  • Engine serial. Internal engine parts must match the engine serial number.
  • Hydraulic cylinder bore. Cylinder seal kit fit depends on bore dimension — measure before ordering.
  • Cab vs. ROPS canopy. Operator station equipment varied between configurations.
