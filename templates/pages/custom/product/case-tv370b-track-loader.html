Where the TV370B fits in the Case skid steer / CTL line

The TV370B sits in Case's alpha-series TV (vertical-lift) compact track loader range between the Case TV370 and the Case TV380. It pairs a tracked undercarriage with vertical-lift loader geometry and Tier 4 Final aftertreatment for emissions compliance.

Engine, hydraulics, and drivetrain

The TV370B runs a Tier 4 Final compact diesel through a hydrostatic dual-path drivetrain on a tracked undercarriage. Loader hydraulics use a load-sensing piston pump with electrohydraulic control valves. Vertical-lift geometry keeps the bucket forward through the lift arc — best matched to truck-loading and stacking applications.

CTL undercarriage life is the largest single recurring cost on these machines. Sprocket, roller, idler, and chain monitoring is critical to keep operating costs predictable. Tier 4 Final aftertreatment adds DPF, DEF, and SCR components that require their own service discipline.

Common service items on the TV370B

  • Engine internals: Tier 4 Final compact diesel - pistons, rings, sleeves, bearings, gaskets. Common-rail high-pressure injection on Tier 4 builds.
  • Tier 4 aftertreatment: DPF service intervals, DEF tank and dosing module, SCR catalyst, EGR cooler service.
  • Fuel system: Common-rail injectors, lift pump, very tight water-separator filtration.
  • Cooling system: Radiator, water pump, thermostat, fan, charge-air cooler, hoses, belts.
  • Hydraulics: Loader lift and bucket tilt cylinder seal kits (86570919 / 86570931 / 86570922 cross-fit on NH/Case alpha-series), piston-pump rebuilds, electrohydraulic control valves.
  • Drivetrain: Hydrostatic dual-path pumps and drive motors, charge pump, filters. CTL undercarriage adds track chains, sprockets, idlers, and rollers.
  • Bucket coupler hardware: 47396814K bucket pin kit cross-fits L216-L230, C232-C238, SR160-SR250, SV185-SV300, TV380, TR270-TR340 - high-volume coupler service kit.
  • Hardware: 191048A2 axle stud & nut cross-fits broadly across the Case skid steer line.
  • Brakes / control: Park brake, control linkage, 48139268 control shock absorber on Case/NH alpha-series control valves.
  • Electrical / ECU: Engine ECU, machine controller, instrument cluster, sensors, wiring harness, telematics module.
  • Cab / operator station: Cab HVAC, seat, gauges, joystick controls, cab glass, 47405956 windshield wiper blade on cab-equipped units.
Undercarriage is the cost center On any CTL, undercarriage wear is the largest single recurring cost over the machine's life. Track tension, sprocket tooth wear, roller seal condition, and idler bearing health all need to be monitored on a regular cadence. Catching an undercarriage issue early on the TV370B is much cheaper than replacing a chain that has worn into a sprocket.

What to verify before ordering

  • Serial number. Confirm the serial on the Case TV370B before ordering year-specific parts.
  • Engine serial. Internal engine parts must match the engine serial number.
  • Aftertreatment configuration. Tier 4 Final aftertreatment configuration varies by build year - confirm DPF and SCR part numbers by serial.
  • Hydraulic cylinder bore. Cylinder seal kit fit depends on bore dimension - measure before ordering.
  • Undercarriage generation. CTL undercarriage components saw revisions across the production run - confirm sprocket, roller, and chain pitch by serial.
  • Cab vs. ROPS canopy. Operator station equipment varied between configurations.

Why the TV370B still earns a paycheck

The Case TV370B is the alpha-series CTL that pairs vertical-lift loader geometry with a tracked undercarriage - flotation for soft material plus forward reach at full height. It is the right machine for truck-loading, stacking, and high-capacity material handling on soft ground where a wheeled skid steer would sink.

Maintenance priorities on a TV370B: clean fuel and tight water separation, DPF regen behavior, hydrostatic charge pressure, and undercarriage wear monitoring (track tension, sprocket and roller wear, idler condition). Stay on top of those and the TV370B is a durable, productive earner across construction, landscape, and ag operations.
