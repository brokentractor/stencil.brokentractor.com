Where the SR250 fits in the Case skid steer line

The SR250 sits in Case’s alpha-series SR (radial-lift) skid steer range between the Case SR240B and the Case SR270. Radial-lift loader geometry, Tier 4 Final aftertreatment, alpha-series cab, electrohydraulic joystick controls. The radial-lift arc keeps the bucket out near the operator’s reach, which is what you want for digging at ground level and loading trucks.

Engine, hydraulics, and drivetrain

The SR250 runs a Tier 4 Final compact diesel through a hydrostatic dual-path drivetrain. Loader hydraulics use a load-sensing piston pump with electrohydraulic control valves and direct-acting cylinders. The radial-lift loader geometry gives a single-arc reach pattern — best matched to ground-level digging, loading trucks, and pallet work where the lift arc keeps the bucket out near the operator’s reach.

Tier 4 Final emissions controls include DPF, SCR with DEF dosing, and cooled EGR. Common-rail fuel injection demands tight water separation and clean fuel — this is the single biggest service variable on the alpha-series engines.

Common service items on the SR250

  • Engine internals: Tier 4 Final compact diesel pistons, rings, sleeves, bearings, gaskets. Common-rail high-pressure injection on Tier 4 builds.
  • Tier 4 aftertreatment: DPF service intervals, DEF tank and dosing module, SCR catalyst, EGR cooler service.
  • Fuel system: Common-rail injectors, lift pump, very tight water-separator filtration.
  • Cooling system: Radiator, water pump, thermostat, fan, charge-air cooler, hoses, belts.
  • Hydraulics: Loader lift and bucket tilt cylinder seal kits (86570919 / 86570931 / 86570922 cross-fit on NH/Case alpha-series), piston-pump rebuilds, electrohydraulic control valves.
  • Drivetrain: Hydrostatic dual-path pumps and drive motors, charge pump, filters.
  • Bucket coupler hardware: 47396814K bucket pin kit cross-fits L216–L230, C232–C238, SR160–SR250, SV185–SV300, TV380, TR270–TR340.
  • Hardware: 191048A2 axle stud & nut cross-fits broadly across the Case skid steer line.
  • Brakes / control: Park brake, control linkage, 48139268 control shock absorber on Case/NH alpha-series control valves.
  • Electrical / ECU: Engine ECU, machine controller, instrument cluster, sensors, wiring harness, telematics module.
  • Cab / operator station: Cab HVAC, seat, gauges, joystick controls, cab glass, 47405956 windshield wiper blade on cab-equipped units.

What to verify before ordering

  • Aftertreatment generation. Tier 4 Final configuration varies by build year — confirm DPF/DEF/SCR layout before ordering.
  • Serial number. Confirm the serial on the Case SR250 before ordering year-specific parts.
  • Engine serial. Internal engine parts must match the engine serial number.
  • Hydraulic cylinder bore. Cylinder seal kit fit depends on bore dimension — measure before ordering.
  • Cab vs. ROPS canopy. Operator station equipment varied between configurations.

Why the SR250 still earns a paycheck

The Case SR250 is an alpha-series radial-lift skid steer covering ground-level dig-and-load duty cycles, loading trucks, pallet work, and any application where the radial-lift geometry’s reach pattern fits the job.

Maintenance priorities on a SR250: clean fuel and tight water separation for common-rail injection, DPF regen behavior, hydrostatic charge pressure, drive-chain tension, and cylinder seal replacement when leaks develop. Broken Tractor maintains parts coverage across the SR alpha-series.
