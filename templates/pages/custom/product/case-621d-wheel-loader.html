Case 621D Wheel Loader: D-Generation Production Wheel Loader

The Case 621D is the D-generation of Case’s 621 production wheel loader - Case diesel, power-shift drivetrain, articulated frame, and the radiator cross-fit (347611A3) that ties the 621D to the 521D/E/F/G family for cooling-system service.

Where the 621D fits in the Case wheel loader line

The 621D is the D-revision of Case’s 621 platform - between the 621C and the 621E. The 347611A3 wheel-loader radiator cross-fits 521D/E/F/G and 621D, simplifying cooling-system parts identification across the mid-size Case wheel loader family.

The 621D carries forward the articulated production wheel loader formula: Case inline diesel, power-shift transmission, planetary final drives, wet-disc service brakes, and a hydraulic loader with electrohydraulic control valves. It sits in the mid-size class between the 521 series and the 721 series.

What changed from the 621C to the 621D

  • Cooling system. The 347611A3 wheel-loader radiator cross-fits the 521D/E/F/G and the 621D - a deliberate parts-commonality move across the mid-size Case wheel loader family.
  • Operator station. Refined cab and operator controls versus the C-series, with updated gauges and improved climate control on cab-equipped units.
  • Electrohydraulic control. Loader hydraulics use a piston pump paired with electrohydraulic control valves.
  • Drivetrain. Power-shift transmission carried forward with refinements, paired with planetary final drives and wet-disc service brakes.
  • Pre-Tier 4. The D-gen predates the Tier 4 aftertreatment requirements that arrived on later F and G machines, keeping the powertrain mechanically simpler to service.
Locating your serial number On the Case 621D the serial is stamped on the frame near the front axle, with a duplicate data plate inside the cab. Confirm the serial before ordering year-specific parts - the 621 platform saw multiple generations and running changes within each.

Engine, hydraulics, and drivetrain

The 621D runs a Case inline diesel through a power-shift transmission. Loader hydraulics use a piston pump with electrohydraulic control valves. Wet-disc service brakes and planetary final drives carry the load. The 347611A3 radiator cross-fit simplifies cooling-system identification across the D-gen and forward in this size class.

Articulated steering uses a pair of steering cylinders working the articulation joint - a high-wear area on production wheel loaders. The G109152 steering cylinder bushing fits 521F, 621, 721, and 821 series machines, another piece of Case parts commonality across the wheel loader range.

Common service items on the 621D

  • Engine internals: Case diesel pistons, rings, sleeves, bearings, gaskets. The R49482 engine motor mount is a high-volume Case diesel service item.
  • Fuel system: Injection pump, injectors, lift pump, primary and secondary filters.
  • Cooling system: 347611A3 radiator (cross-fit 521D/E/F/G and 621D), water pump, thermostat, fan, hoses, belts.
  • Loader hydraulics: Cylinder seal kits, pump rebuilds, control valves, hoses.
  • Articulation steering: Steering cylinder seal kits, articulation pivot pins and bushings. G109152 steering cylinder bushing fits 521F, 621, 721, 821 series.
  • Loader components: Bucket teeth, cutting edges, bushings, pivot pins.
  • Drivetrain: Power-shift clutch packs, transmission, axle seals, planetary final drives, transmission shift lever assembly.
  • Brakes: Wet-disc service brakes. S300718 brake shoe, A12985 slave cylinder, and N8695 master cylinder are recurring service items.
  • Electrical: Starter, alternator, wiring harness, gauges, switches.
  • Operator station: Seat, gauges, control levers, cab parts. 47880728 cab fresh-air filter on cab-equipped units.

What to verify before ordering

  • Generation matters. The 621-series saw multiple generations (B, C, D, E, F, G) - confirm by serial before ordering.
  • Serial number. Confirm the serial on the frame near the front axle (or the data plate inside the cab) before ordering year-specific parts.
  • Engine serial. Case diesel internal parts must match the engine serial.
  • Axle make. Some 621-series machines shipped with ZF axles - confirm axle make before ordering brake-disc and final-drive kits.
  • Cab vs. ROPS canopy. Operator station equipment varied. The 47880728 cab filter only applies to cab-equipped units.
  • Radiator cross-fit. The 347611A3 radiator covers 521D/E/F/G and 621D, but confirm fan and shroud configuration on your machine.

Why the 621D still earns a paycheck

The 621D is the D-revision Case production wheel loader. Pre-Tier 4 emissions keep the powertrain mechanically simpler than later F and G machines, the cab and operator controls were refined versus the C-series, and the 347611A3 radiator cross-fit simplifies fleet-wide cooling-system management for shops running both the 521 and 621 series.

Maintenance priorities on a 621D: clean fuel and quality filtration, cooling-system service (347611A3 radiator service interval), hydraulic seal replacement, articulation pivot service, and brake service. Broken Tractor maintains parts coverage across the 621 family.
