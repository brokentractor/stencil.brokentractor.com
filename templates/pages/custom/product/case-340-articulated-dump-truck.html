Where the 340 fits in the Case articulated dump truck line

The Case 340 is the largest Case Articulated Dump Truck — above the 330B in payload, chassis size, and hauling capacity. Heavy-class 6x6 ADT for major earthmoving, mining-edge, and infrastructure work where the 330B runs out of payload.

Engine, hydraulics, and drivetrain

The 340 runs a Cummins heavy-duty diesel through a ZF planetary automatic transmission into a 6x6 drivetrain — front axle plus a rear bogie. Articulated chassis pivots between the front truck and the rear bogie / dump body. Hoist hydraulics raise the heavy-class dump body. 8603163 brake pin cross-fits Case 521–921 wheel loaders and 335–340B Articulated Dump Trucks — a broad Case brake-pin service item.

Common service items on the 340

  • Engine internals: Cummins heavy-duty diesel pistons, rings, sleeves, bearings, head gaskets, full overhaul kits.
  • Cooling system: Radiator, water pump, thermostat, fan, hoses, belts, charge-air cooler. Heavy-class radiator capacity matched to the 340’s engine.
  • Transmission: ZF planetary clutch packs, transmission filters and oil, torque converter service.
  • Drivetrain / axles: Front axle, rear bogie axles, planetary final drives, driveshafts. 8603527 axle cover O-ring cross-fits Case 521–1021 loaders and 325–330B ADTs (also applicable to 340 axle service depending on configuration).
  • Hoist hydraulics: Heavy-class dump body hoist cylinder seal kits, hoist pump rebuild, control valve service.
  • Articulation: Front-to-rear articulation pivot pins, bushings, steering cylinder seal kits.
  • Brakes: Wet-disc heavy service brakes with retarder service. 8603163 brake pin is the direct-fit cross-fit brake-pin service item (335–340B + Case 521–921 loaders).
  • Tires + wheels: Heavy off-highway radials, rim and wheel-bearing service.
  • Cab and electrical: Cab HVAC, ECU, instrument cluster, sensors, wiring harness, telematics.

What to verify before ordering

  • Serial number. Confirm the serial on the main frame near the articulation joint or on the cab data plate before ordering year-specific parts.
  • Engine serial. Cummins engine internal and aftertreatment parts are calibrated to specific engine serials.
  • ZF transmission revision. ZF planetary transmissions saw revisions during production — confirm by serial.
  • Brake pin range. 8603163 brake pin fits the broad Case 521–921 wheel loader + 335–340B ADT range; confirm pin position before ordering.
  • Hoist cylinder bore. Heavy-class hoist cylinder seal kit fit depends on bore dimension — measure before ordering.

Why the 340 still earns a paycheck

The Case 340 is the largest Case articulated dump truck — heavy-class earthmoving where on-highway trucks can’t go and the 330B runs out of payload. Major mining-edge work, large infrastructure projects, quarries, and high-volume construction. 6x6 articulated configuration gives soft-ground hauling capability that wheeled trucks can’t deliver.

Maintenance priorities on a 340: clean fuel and quality filtration on the Cummins, ZF transmission oil quality and clutch-pack service, hoist hydraulic system condition, articulation pivot lubrication, brake-pin service (8603163 cross-fit), and heavy-class cooling and tire monitoring.
