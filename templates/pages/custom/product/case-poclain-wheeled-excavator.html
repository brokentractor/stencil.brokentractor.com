Where the Poclain 81P Cruz-Air fits in the Case excavator line

The Poclain 81P Cruz-Air is part of Case’s legacy wheeled excavator lineage - a predecessor to the modern CX-series excavator line. It sits between the 125B Excavator and the 1085B Cruz-Air in the legacy sequence, on a wheeled chassis with hydrostatic dual-path drive and outrigger or dozer-blade stabilization.

Engine, hydraulics, and drivetrain

The Poclain 81P Cruz-Air runs a Case or Cummins diesel through a hydrostatic dual-path drivetrain. Pre-Tier 4 emissions - mechanical injection on the earliest builds, electronic engine control on later production. The main hydraulic pump drives boom, dipper, bucket, swing, and propel through mechanical or electrohydraulic control valves. The wheeled chassis carries tire-and-axle drivetrain components with outrigger or dozer-blade stabilization.

Service-friendly architecture: accessible hydraulic plumbing, mechanical-feel controls on earlier builds, and a swing system built on a serviceable bearing and reduction gear that rewards regular grease intervals.

Common service items on the Poclain 81P Cruz-Air

  • Engine internals: Pistons, rings, sleeves, bearings, head gaskets. Mechanical-injection or early-electronic depending on production year.
  • Fuel system: Injection pump, injectors, lift pump, primary and secondary fuel filters.
  • Cooling system: Radiator, water pump, thermostat, fan, hoses, belts.
  • Hydraulic system: Boom, dipper, bucket, and swing cylinder seal kits, main pump rebuild, control valves, hoses.
  • Boom and dipper: Pin and bushing service at every joint, bucket pins, thumb attachment hardware, quick-coupler service.
  • Wheeled chassis: Tires, wheel bearings, axle and final drive service, suspension components if equipped.
  • Swing system: Swing motor and reduction gear service, swing bearing inspection, swing brake.
  • Electrical: Starter, alternator, wiring harness, gauges, switches.
  • Cab glass and wiper: KHN16820 24-inch wiper blade, KHN3046 front lower glass, and KHN2632 front upper glass cross-fit across Case and Link-Belt excavators.
  • Operator station: Seat, joystick controls, pedal linkage, instrument panel, cab HVAC.
