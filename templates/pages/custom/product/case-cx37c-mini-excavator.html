Where the CX37C fits in the Case excavator line

The CX37C is a 3.7-ton class C-Series mini excavator sized for utility, landscape, and contractor work at the upper end of the small-mini class. It sits in the compact end of the Case excavator family alongside other C-Series mini machines, with rubber-track flotation and a footprint that fits jobs where a larger tracked excavator won’t.

Engine, hydraulics, and drivetrain

The CX37C runs a small Tier 4 Final compact diesel into a hydrostatic dual-path drivetrain with a rubber-track undercarriage. Boom, dipper, bucket, swing, and propel functions run from a single-section main hydraulic pump with electrohydraulic or mechanical control depending on configuration.

Common-rail injection on the Tier 4 Final engine demands clean fuel and tight water separation. Aftertreatment includes a diesel particulate filter (DPF) with periodic regeneration and a selective catalytic reduction (SCR) catalyst with DEF dosing for NOx control. EGR cooling rounds out the emissions package.

Common service items on the CX37C

  • Engine internals: Compact Tier 4 Final diesel pistons, rings, sleeves, head gaskets, common-rail injection components.
  • Tier 4 Final aftertreatment: DPF service intervals and regeneration behavior, DEF tank and dosing module, SCR catalyst, EGR cooler service.
  • Fuel system: Common-rail injectors, lift pump, primary and secondary filters with tight water-separator standards.
  • Cooling system: Radiator, water pump, thermostat, fan, charge-air cooler on turbo builds, hoses, belts.
  • Hydraulic system: Boom, dipper, bucket, and swing cylinder seal kits, main pump rebuild, electrohydraulic control valves, hoses.
  • Boom and dipper: Pin and bushing service at every joint, bucket pins, thumb attachment hardware, quick-coupler service.
  • Undercarriage: Rubber track inspection and replacement, rollers, idlers, drive sprockets, recoil springs.
  • Swing system: Swing motor and reduction gear service, swing bearing inspection, swing brake.
  • Electrical / ECU: Engine ECU, instrument cluster, sensors, harness, telematics module on equipped units.
  • Cab glass and wiper: KHN16820 24-inch wiper blade, KHN3046 front lower glass, and KHN2632 front upper glass cross-fit across Case and Link-Belt excavators.
  • Operator station: Seat, joystick controls, pedal linkage, instrument panel, cab HVAC.

What to verify before ordering

  • Machine serial number. Confirm the serial on the CX37C before ordering year-specific parts - the C-Series saw running revisions through production.
  • Engine serial. Engine internal parts must match the engine serial number.
  • Hydraulic cylinder bore. Cylinder seal kit fit depends on bore dimension - measure before ordering.
  • Tier 4 emissions revision. DPF and DEF/SCR components saw revisions during production - confirm by serial.
  • Engine ECU calibration. Common-rail injectors and ECU sensors are calibrated to specific build years.
  • Bucket and quick coupler. Quick-coupler interface varies - confirm before ordering attachment-side hardware.

Why the CX37C still earns a paycheck

The Case CX37C is sized for compact-excavator duty cycles where a 3.7-ton machine is the right tool - residential landscape, utility, irrigation, plumbing, and any tight-quarters digging where larger excavators won’t fit. Modern Tier 4 Final emissions compliance, compact footprint, and rubber-track flotation suit residential and contractor work where a wheeled or larger tracked machine would mark up turf or fail to fit.

Maintenance priorities on a CX37C: clean fuel and tight water separation for common-rail injection, DPF regen behavior tracking, hydraulic oil at temperature with clean filtration, rubber-track tension and wear monitoring, and pin and bushing service at every working joint.
