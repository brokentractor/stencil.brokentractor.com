Where the 210G LC fits in the JD excavator line

The 210G LC sits above the 200 G-Tier and below the 245 P-Tier in the modern JD lineup. 21-ton class with the long-carriage option for added stability on lift and reach. Strong direct-fit service-part availability - multiple critical components are 210G LC direct fit.

Locating your serial number On the John Deere 210G LC the serial number is typically located on a metal plate on the left side of the frame near the boom base or under the operator’s cab; it’s also stamped into the upper structure frame. Confirm the serial before ordering parts.

Engine, hydraulics, and undercarriage

The 210G LC runs a JD Tier 4 Final diesel through a hydrostatic drive into a long-carriage steel-track undercarriage. Multiple direct-fit service parts are available at Broken Tractor: AT446539 radiator (210G + 210GLC direct fit), AT421243 heat exchanger (210G + 210GLC), AT380314 oil cooler (210D/210G/210GLC), PG200154 swing bearing (210DLC + 210GLC), and 9233692 final drive (200D/200D LC/200G/210G/225D).

Common service items on the 210G LC

  • Engine: JD Tier 4 Final diesel internals.
  • Aftertreatment: DPF, DEF, SCR catalyst.
  • Cooling - Radiator: AT446539 radiator direct fit.
  • Cooling - Heat Exchanger: AT421243 heat exchanger direct fit.
  • Cooling - Oil Cooler: AT380314 oil cooler (210D/210G/210GLC).
  • Hydraulics: Main pump, valve, motors, cylinders.
  • Final drive: 9233692 final drive cross-fit.
  • Swing: PG200154 swing bearing (210DLC + 210GLC).
  • Undercarriage: LC steel tracks, shoes, idlers, rollers.

What to verify before ordering

  • Serial number. Confirm the serial on the frame near the boom base before ordering year-specific parts.
  • Engine + aftertreatment serial. Tier 4 Final emissions parts are serial-specific.
  • Cooling parts cross-fit. AT446539 + AT421243 fit 210G + 210GLC; AT380314 adds 210D.
  • Swing bearing. PG200154 fits 210DLC + 210GLC.
  • Final drive cross-fit. 9233692 fits 200D/200D LC/200G/210G/225D.
  • LC vs standard. Confirm carriage configuration before ordering undercarriage parts.

Why the 210G LC still earns a paycheck

The 210G LC stays in service because the 21-ton LC class hits the production sweet spot for commercial construction - enough size and lift to do real work without crossing into the operating-cost territory of 25+ ton machines. Strong direct-fit service-part availability (cooling, swing, final drive all stocked) makes ownership economics work.

Maintenance priorities on a 210G LC: clean fuel and DEF, aftertreatment service, cooling system service (AT446539 radiator + AT421243 heat exchanger + AT380314 oil cooler - three direct-fit service parts), hydraulic oil cleanliness, swing bearing grease (PG200154 direct fit), final-drive oil (9233692 cross-fit), and LC undercarriage wear. Broken Tractor maintains the full 210G LC service profile.
