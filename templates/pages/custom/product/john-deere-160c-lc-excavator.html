Where the 160C LC fits in the John Deere excavator line

The 160C LC is a mid-class C-Series JD excavator with long-carriage undercarriage for added stability. It sits above the 120C and below the 200C LC in the C-Series mid range. The shared JD/Hitachi chassis means broad parts availability across both the JD parts network and the Hitachi EX150 / EX160 / ZX160 platform.

Engine, hydraulics, and undercarriage

The 160C LC runs a JD diesel through a hydraulic main pump into a long-carriage steel-track undercarriage. Direct-fit service parts available at Broken Tractor: AP33359 radiator (110 + 120 + 160LC), AT317587 oil cooler (160C LC + 120C), PV7001 shim & seal kit (160C-160G + Hitachi EX150-ZX160), 4602562 upper windshield, and 4602563R lower windshield.

The long-carriage undercarriage is the defining feature versus a standard carriage 160C - the longer track frame adds the lateral stability needed for over-the-side lift and reach work. UC components (idlers, rollers, sprocket, tracks, shoes) are LC-specific and must be ordered against that configuration.

Common service items on the 160C LC

  • Engine: JD diesel internals - pistons, rings, sleeves, bearings, head gaskets, overhaul kits.
  • Cooling - Radiator: AP33359 radiator (110/120/160LC direct fit).
  • Cooling - Oil Cooler: AT317587 oil cooler (120C + 160C LC).
  • Hydraulics: PV7001 shim & seal kit covers 160C-160G plus Hitachi EX150-ZX160. Main pump, valve, swing/travel motors, cylinders.
  • Cab glass: 4602562 upper and 4602563R lower.
  • Undercarriage (long carriage): Steel tracks, shoes, idlers, rollers, sprocket - LC configuration.
  • Swing: Swing bearing, swing motor, swing gearbox.
  • Fuel system: Injectors, lift pump, fuel filters, water separator.
  • Electrical: Starter, alternator, harnesses, switches, sensors.

What to verify before ordering

  • Serial number. Confirm the serial on the upper frame before ordering year-specific parts.
  • LC vs standard. 160C LC has the long-carriage undercarriage - confirm UC configuration before ordering UC parts.
  • Hydraulic seal kit family. PV7001 covers 160C through 160G plus Hitachi EX150-ZX160.
  • Radiator cross-fit. AP33359 fits 110 + 120 + 160LC.
  • Oil cooler cross-fit. AT317587 fits 120C + 160C LC.
  • Cab glass position. Confirm upper vs lower vs door position before ordering.

Why the 160C LC still earns a paycheck

The 160C LC stays in service because 16-ton long-carriage mid-class excavators are the workhorse of commercial site prep, infrastructure work, utility deep-trench, and demolition. The LC option adds the stability needed for over-the-side lift and reach work that a standard carriage can’t handle as effectively.

Maintenance priorities on a 160C LC: clean fuel on the JD diesel, hydraulic oil cleanliness (PV7001 seal kit family), radiator service (AP33359 direct fit), oil cooler service (AT317587), swing bearing grease, long-carriage undercarriage wear monitoring, and cab glass (4602562 / 4602563R direct fit).
