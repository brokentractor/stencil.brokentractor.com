Where the 100C fits in the IH / Dresser line

The IH 100C is a mid-size International Harvester crawler loader and is the direct predecessor to the IH 100E. The platform shares a great deal of running gear with its larger sibling, the 125 loader, and the TD7/TD8 dozers - notably steering-brake packs and seat cushions. That cross-fit is the single most useful fact when sourcing service parts for one of these machines: many components that read “TD7/TD8” on the box drop into a 100C without modification.

Locating your serial number On the IH 100C the serial number plate is on the operator-platform ID plate. Capture both the machine serial and the engine serial before ordering - IH-era and Dresser-era machines saw multiple running changes that affect internal engine parts and brake-pack revisions.

Engine, hydraulics, and drivetrain

The IH 100C runs an IH diesel through a mechanical drivetrain into a tracked undercarriage. Loader hydraulics drive bucket lift and tilt. The implement hydraulic circuit on these machines is straightforward gear-pump-fed work-section plumbing - reliable when fed clean fluid, predictable to service.

Steering is by independent track braking - the signature IH/Dresser steering-brake design used across the TD7/TD8 dozer family and the 100/125 loader siblings. Brake-pack service is one of the most common service items on these machines and the parts are well supported through the cross-fit DBSKIT family.

Common service items on the 100C

  • Engine internals: IH / Dresser diesel pistons, rings, sleeves, head gaskets. The S.57678 head gasket set cross-fits the IH/Dresser diesel family.
  • Cooling system: The 3132738R93 water pump fits IH / Dresser dozers and crawler loaders.
  • Hydraulic system: 623084C92 main hydraulic pump (Dresser cross-fit), plus loader bucket lift / tilt cylinder reseal service.
  • Steering brakes: The DBSKIT basic steering brake kit and DBSKIT-2 brake shoe kit are TD7/TD8 / 100/125 cross-fit brake service items. Add the 621037C1 brake shoe for individual shoe replacement.
  • Operator station: The PV862 seat package fits IH / Dresser dozers and loaders. The 621619C1 bottom seat cushion fits 100/125 loaders and TD7/TD8 dozers.
  • Bucket / loader: Cutting edges, teeth, pins and bushings, loader arm pivot service.
  • Undercarriage: Track chains, rollers, idlers, sprockets, recoil springs.
  • Electrical: Starter, alternator, gauges, switches.

What to verify before ordering

  • Serial number. Confirm the serial on the 100C before ordering year-specific parts.
  • Engine serial. Internal engine parts must match the engine serial number.
  • Generation. IH-era and Dresser-era machines saw multiple revisions - confirm by serial.
  • Undercarriage spec. Track shoe count, roller and idler configuration vary by build - measure before ordering.
  • Brake-pack revision. Confirm whether the machine takes the DBSKIT or DBSKIT-2 pattern before ordering shoes or kits.

Why the 100C still earns a paycheck

The 100C is the workhorse IH mid-size crawler loader - construction yards, farm operations, and contractor work. The cross-fit relationship with the TD7/TD8 dozer family means parts coverage on the brake pack, seat package, and engine cooling components is far better than the production volume on the 100C alone would suggest.

Maintenance priorities on a 100C: clean fuel, steering brake service (DBSKIT + 621037C1 cross-fit), loader cylinder reseal service when the bucket starts to drift, and undercarriage monitoring before chain stretch begins eating sprockets.
