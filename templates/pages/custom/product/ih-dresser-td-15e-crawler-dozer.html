Where the TD-15E fits in the IH / Dresser line

The TD-15E is the E-series Dresser TD-15 - a heavy-duty crawler dozer purpose-built for large-scale earthmoving. The TD-15 family originated with International Harvester and continued under Dresser after the IH construction equipment line was sold off. The E-generation refined the heavy-class TD-15 platform with updated hydraulics, an improved operator station, and revised undercarriage components, and was eventually succeeded by the TD-15G.

In the Dresser hierarchy, the TD-15 sits between the mid-size TD-9 and TD-12 platforms and the larger TD-20 / TD-25 heavy-class machines. Operators run TD-15Es for grading, land clearing, mining-support roles, and large site-prep work.

Engine, hydraulics, and drivetrain

The TD-15E runs a heavy IH / Dresser diesel through a hydrostatic transmission. The heavy-class chassis, blade, and undercarriage are sized for large-scale earthmoving - this is not a finish-grade machine, it’s a production crawler for moving serious volumes of material.

The hydraulic circuit drives blade lift, tilt, and angle functions (depending on blade configuration) and feeds the steering brake hydraulics. The undercarriage uses heavy-class sealed-and-lubricated track chain with TD-15-pattern rollers, idlers, sprockets, and recoil springs.

Common service items on the TD-15E

  • Engine internals: IH / Dresser heavy diesel - pistons, rings, sleeves, head gaskets. The S.57678 head gasket set cross-fits the IH / Dresser engine family.
  • Cooling system: The 3132738R93 water pump cross-fits IH / Dresser dozers including the TD-15E.
  • Hydraulic system: Main hydraulic pump (623084C92), 620630C92 blade angle cylinder, blade lift and tilt cylinder seal service.
  • Steering brakes: Heavy-duty wet-disc brake service - friction discs, separator plates, seals.
  • Operator station: PV862 seat package for IH / Dresser dozers and loaders.
  • Blade: Cutting edges, mounting hardware (5J4773 cutting edge bolt), pivot pins, bushings.
  • Undercarriage: Heavy-class track chains, rollers, idlers, sprockets, recoil springs.
  • Electrical: Starter, alternator, gauges, switches, harnesses.

What to verify before ordering

  • Serial number. Confirm the machine serial on the TD-15E before ordering year-specific parts. The TD-15 saw multiple revisions across the IH and Dresser eras.
  • Engine serial. Internal engine parts must match the engine serial number, not just the machine serial.
  • Generation. IH-era and Dresser-era TD-15 machines used different component part numbers - confirm by serial.
  • Undercarriage spec. Track shoe count, roller and idler configuration vary by build - measure before ordering.
  • Blade configuration. Straight, angle, and semi-U blades have different cylinder and mounting hardware.

Why the TD-15E still earns a paycheck

The TD-15E is the heavy-duty Dresser TD-15 that customers still run for large-scale grading, land clearing, and site prep. The IH / Dresser heavy-diesel platform and hydrostatic drive are proven across decades of production-class earthmoving, and parts coverage remains strong because the TD-15 family shared component lineage with other IH and Dresser machines.

Maintenance priorities on a TD-15E: clean fuel, regular hydrostatic system service, heavy-class undercarriage monitoring, and blade and pivot pin service. Broken Tractor maintains parts coverage for the TD-15E alongside the broader IH / Dresser crawler family.
