Where the 584 (Pre-C) fits in the Case forklift line

The 584 (pre-C) is the foundational model in the Case RT forklift line — predecessor to the 584C and all subsequent lettered variants. Original mechanical-injection diesel, power shuttle, fork lift mast with hydraulic tilt and lift. Built simple, built to last.

Locating your serial number On the Case 584 (pre-C) the serial number is stamped on the frame near the operator platform, with a duplicate data plate on or near the operator console. As an early-production vintage model, the 584 saw multiple revisions during its short production window.

Engine, hydraulics, and drivetrain

The original 584 runs a Case mechanical-injection diesel through a power shuttle transmission. Power-shuttle clutch packs in forward and reverse drive the wheels through mechanical front and rear axles. Mast lift and tilt are hydraulic — single-section pump, mechanical control valves, direct-acting cylinders. Pre-electronic, pre-emissions — service architecture rewards a wrench and a parts catalog.

Common service items on the 584 (Pre-C)

  • Engine internals: Case mechanical-injection diesel: pistons, rings, sleeves, head gaskets, bearings.
  • Fuel system: Mechanical injection pump, injectors, lift pump, primary and secondary filters.
  • Cooling system: Radiator, water pump, thermostat, fan, hoses, belts.
  • Power shuttle / transmission: Power shuttle clutch packs, forward/reverse modulation, transmission filters and oil. Power-shuttle is a defining feature on Case RT forklifts.
  • Steering hydraulics: Steering motor, steering cylinder seal kit, control valve, hoses.
  • Mast hydraulics: Lift cylinder seal kits, tilt cylinder seal kits, chain tensioner, control valves, hoses.
  • Mast components: Mast carriage rollers, lift chain inspection and replacement, fork carriage bushings, side-shift attachment service.
  • Drivetrain: Front and rear axles, planetary final drives, driveshaft, axle differential service. 175978A1 needle bearing 10-pack and K395075 thrust washer fit 585G / 586G / 588G + loader-backhoe.
  • Brakes: A168790 parking brake disc cross-fits Case forklift / loader-backhoe / trencher applications — high-volume service item. Service brakes and brake hydraulics.
  • Cab / operator station: Seat, joystick / lever controls, gauges, key switch, ROPS canopy or enclosed cab. 377594A5 cab air filter fits Case loader tractor / backhoe / forklift cabs.
  • Electrical: Starter, alternator, wiring harness, gauges, switches.

What to verify before ordering

  • Serial number. Confirm the serial on the Case 584 (Pre-C) forklift before ordering year-specific parts.
  • Engine serial. Internal engine parts must match the engine serial number.
  • Mast configuration. Lift height, carriage width, fork length, and side-shift options vary — confirm mast spec before ordering carriage / chain / cylinder parts.
  • Two-wheel vs. four-wheel drive. Some Case RT forklift models are available in 2WD and 4WD configurations — drivetrain parts differ.
  • Power-shuttle generation. Power-shuttle transmissions saw revisions — confirm by serial before ordering clutch-pack components.

Why the 584 (Pre-C) still earns a paycheck

The Case 584 (pre-C) is a vintage workhorse — the model that defined Case RT forklifts for half a century of production. Lumberyards, farm operations, light construction, and any application where rough-terrain forklift capability was needed but a modern emissions-equipped forklift wasn’t practical or appropriate.

Maintenance priorities on a pre-C 584: clean fuel through good filtration, power-shuttle oil at temperature, mast chain inspection and lift cylinder seal replacement, brake service (A168790 cross-fits the Case family), and engine service per the original Case schedule.
