Shipping & Returns

Most parts ship the same day from warehouses across the country — and if something's not right, we'll make it right. Here's how it works.

Shipping

Need your part quickly? You're in luck. We have warehouses all over the country to keep delivery fast — most parts ship from our warehouses the same day you place your order, and many orders arrive the next business day.

Our in-house parts are manufactured to strict quality standards in our Ohio warehouse. Hydraulic cylinders, pins, bushings, sheet metal, track adjusters, booms, and dipper sticks are all ready to ship the same day they're purchased — less time waiting on a shipment, more time back to work.

A few shipping policies to know

  • Additional shipping charges may be incurred if an incorrect or incomplete address is provided with your order.
  • Unfortunately, we can't ship to P.O. boxes.
  • Expedited shipping is available for an extra fee as a service to our customers — expedited transit times, however, are not guaranteed or warrantied.
  • All shipments 150 lbs and above have a lift gate charge option. You must be present at the time of delivery if a lift gate is required.

Returns & Exchanges

Accidentally purchased the incorrect part? It happens — and we're happy to help you return it or swap it for the right one. Every return needs a return authorization number before it ships back, so start with the request form or give us a call.

Before you ship anything back
  • Please do not refuse a shipment unless the parts were damaged in shipping. Refused shipments get a 30% restocking fee, regardless of the reason for refusal.
  • Glass is not returnable — please check measurements prior to ordering.
  • Hydraulic cylinder seal kits are not returnable once the package is opened.
  • Any opened kit is subject to a 20% restocking fee. This applies to seal kits, filter kits, transmission kits, and similar.

Who pays return shipping?

  • The part is defective
